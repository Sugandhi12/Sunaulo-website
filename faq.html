Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a Booking?

We accept bookings online through our online booking diary, over the phone or via email to reservations@sunaulo.com.np On completion of a booking you will receive a confirmation email with lots of useful information about your visit. If you do not receive a confirmation email within 24 hours of making your booking please contact the restaurant directly so we can make sure your booking is in our system. For groups larger than 8 people, please call the restaurant directly on 01-4116287.


What is your cancellation policy?

During the reservation process you will be asked to enter the details of a valid credit card. We operate a 48 hour cancellation policy and will not charge the card unless you should cancel with less than 48 hours' notice in which case a Rs500 cancellation charge will be applied.


Is the restaurant accessible?

Yes, The restaurant building is accessible from the street to wheelchair users. We also have disable toilets and we also have elevators.


What sort of food is served at Sunaulo?

We serve a variety of food items which include all the local and Nepali cuisines. We also have chinese, italian, indian and koreans food. We have different food and drinks. All foods are very hygienic and cooked by professional chefs.


Are children allowed?

Yes we welcome children of all ages, however, we do have any special menus or smaller dishes available for them.


Are you hiring?

We are always looking for waitresses who are hard working and have some experience. Please send us your CV to careers@sunaulo.com.np if you are interested in joining the team!
